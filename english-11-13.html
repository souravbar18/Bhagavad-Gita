॥ Chapter 11, Shloka 13 ॥

तत्रैकस्थं जगत्कृत्स्नं प्रविभक्तमनेकधा |
अपश्यद्देवदेवस्य शरीरे पाण्डवस्तदा || ११.१३ ||

Summary Translation:

At that time Arjuna could see in the universal form of the Lord the unlimited expansions of the universe situated in one place although divided into many, many thousands.

Detailed Analysis:

Sanjaya identifies the 'Theoretic Integration of the Divided Universe.' He says, 'tatraika-sthaṁ jagat kṛitsnaṁ pravibhaktam anekadhā'—there, situated in one (eka-stham), the entire (kritsnam) universe, though divided (pravibhaktam) in many ways (anekadhā). This analysis focuses on the 'Theoretic Paradox of Unified Multiplicity.' Usually, we see things as 'Separate'—planets, stars, mountains, people. But Arjuna sees them all 'situated in one place'—'apaśhyad deva-devasya śharīre' (within the body of the God of gods). This detailed analysis reveals that Sanjaya defines 'The Body of God' as the ' Field' in which all 'Sub-fields' of existence exist. This shloka provides a ' Framework for Holistic Perception.' It teaches that 'Diversity' is not the opposite of 'Unity,' but a 'Mode' of it. Sanjaya is telling the King that 'The entire jigsaw puzzle of the universe is now seen as a single picture within Krishna.' This is the ' Goal of the Synthetic Seeker,' where 'The Many' are 'One'.

The detailed analysis of 'Pravibhaktam' (Divided) is the 'Theoretic Description of Cosmic Categorization.' In our practical lives, we see barriers between things. Krishna is stating that those barriers are 'Internal' to His own being. Krishna is defining 'The Self' as the 'Common Denominator.' This shloka provides a 'Framework for the Structure of Reality.' It teaches that 'Deva-devasya' (God of gods) implies that even the rulers of various worlds are 'Parts' of this one body. Sanjaya is defining 'Tadā' (At that time) as the ' Moment of Epiphany.' This verse is the ' Proof that the Lord is the glue of the cosmos.' By showing the 'Divided' as 'Unified,' he covers the 'Architecture' of the vision. This shloka is the 'Universal Manual for Seeing Connection,' forcing us to realize that 'Nothing is truly isolated, for everything breathes within the Divine Body'. This is the ' Goal of the Connected Seeker,' where 'The Universe' is 'Localized'.
